Our Process:

Education

Our staff is educated about your school/department and determines the best approach for a campaign.

Lead Development

We target what we believe to be the best potential leads for advertisers/sponsors and start to roll reach out to our national corporate sponsors.

Campaign

Our expert staff of 25+ sponsor reps starts to reach out and acquire local and corporate sponsors/advertisers.

Product Design & Development

Our designers and developers begin to produce the products and ad artwork.

Proofing

All products are sent to you prior to production to ensure accuracy and satisfaction.

Account Reconciliation

Any sponsors/advertisers with balances are reconciled.

Shipping

Products are shipped to the school and sponsors.

Funding

A financial return check is sent in the mail to you to help fund your teams.
